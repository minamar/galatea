Training session instructions


Thank you for filling in the questionnaire. Now you can start a short training session.

Use the play button (bottom left of the next screen) for the robot to execute an expression.

You can replay the expression once, if you need to see it again.

***

After you have watched the expression, you can use the sliding bars to give your ratings:

Pleasure (how negative or positive was the robot's expression) and Arousal (how tired or excited was the robot's expression).

Submit when you are ready and then select the difficulty level in the scale that will appear.
